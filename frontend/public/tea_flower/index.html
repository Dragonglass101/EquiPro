Our Purpose

This web app is designed to do is to streamline the process of issuing sports equipment to students and make it hassle-free.

One can get a basketball, football or for that matter any sports equipment in a matter of minutes by but a few clicks. Our app makes sure that you can see a live list of the current owners of any sports equipment which means you can borrow from your friends without ever having to wait.
